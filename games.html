The call sheet

Every game with enough independent streamers behind it to trust the numbers, ranked by opportunity: audience size, how under-saturated it is, and its recent growth. Click a card for its own best day/hour and Steam catalog info.

No games match those filters. Try widening the search.

Methodology. Opportunity score blends audience size (40%), viewers-per-streamer (35%) and clipped growth (25%), then discounts games backed by fewer than ~15 distinct streamers so a single person's lucky night can't dominate the ranking. Best day/hour is shown in whichever mode you pick inside a game's detail card — standardized (UTC) or time-of-day (streamer-local, estimated from broadcast language).
